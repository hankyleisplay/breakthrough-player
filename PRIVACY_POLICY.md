# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

hankyledevteam ("we", "us", "our") provides hankyledevteam Player+ ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## 1. Data We Collect

By default, the App is designed for local media playback. We may process:

- License information entered by user (product key)
- Basic app settings stored locally (theme, playback preferences)
- Local playback metadata (resume positions by filename)

We do not upload local media files to our servers by default.

## 2. How Data Is Used

We use data to:

- Validate and apply license status
- Save user preferences and playback continuity
- Improve reliability and user experience

## 3. Data Storage

Most app data is stored locally on the user device (for example via browser/electron local storage).

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data only:

- When required by law
- To protect legal rights and prevent fraud
- With service providers strictly necessary for payment, licensing, or support

## 5. Security

We apply reasonable technical measures, but no system is completely secure.

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on local law, users may request access, correction, or deletion of personal data we control.

## 7. Children

The App is not intended for children under applicable legal age thresholds in your region.

## 8. International Users

If services are provided across regions, data may be processed in countries with different legal protections.
