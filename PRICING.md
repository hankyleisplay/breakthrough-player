# Pricing Plan

## Recommended Public Pricing

- Free: $0
- Pro (One-time): $29
- Studio Pack (One-time): $79

## Plan Details

### Free

- Core playback
- Playlist basics
- Theme switch
- Keyboard shortcuts

### Pro ($29)

- Everything in Free
- Mini Player mode
- Subtitle support
- Full EQ panel and presets
- Priority feature updates

### Studio Pack ($79)

- Everything in Pro
- Commercial usage rights for one business entity
- Priority support email
- Early access to beta features

## Discount Strategy

- Launch week: 20% off Pro
- Upgrade path: Free -> Pro in-app upsell
- Bundle: Pro + next major version pass

## Suggested Refund Policy

- 7-day refund window for first purchase
- No refund after key abuse or chargeback history

## Notes

- Keep pricing in USD globally for simpler operations.
- Add regional pricing later if conversion drops in specific regions.
